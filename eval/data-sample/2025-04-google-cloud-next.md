# Highlights from Ai2 at Google Cloud Next

**Date:** April 2025  
**Source:** [Allen Institute for AI](https://allenai.org/blog/ai2-google-next)

## Summary

Ai2 participated in Google Cloud Next 2025, highlighting several key initiatives including partnerships, new tools, and major investments.

## Key Announcements

### 1. Partnership with Google Cloud

Ai2 announced a collaboration with Google Cloud to integrate its suite of open AI models into Google Cloud's Vertex AI Model Garden:
- **Models included**: OLMo, TÃ¼lu, and Molmo
- **Goal**: Easier access for developers and organizations to build with open AI models
- **Focus**: Faster and more responsible AI innovation, particularly in sectors where trust and data privacy are paramount

### 2. Introduction of OLMoTrace

Ai2 unveiled OLMoTrace, a tool designed to enhance transparency in language models:
- Allows users to trace model output back to original training data
- Integrated into the Ai2 Playground
- Enables exploration of how open models like OLMo 2 generate responses
- Significant step toward building more trustworthy AI systems

### 3. Fireside Chat on AI-Powered Research

Ai2 CEO Ali Farhadi joined Google Public Sector's Karen Dahut to discuss:
- How open-source AI can transform research and innovation
- Importance of transparency and accessibility in AI
- Role of tools like OLMoTrace in building confidence in AI systems

### 4. $20 Million Investment in Cancer AI Alliance

Ai2 and Google Cloud committed $20 million to advance AI-powered research for the Cancer AI Alliance (CAIA):
- Consortium focused on accelerating cancer research
- Uses open, privacy-preserving AI
- Demonstrates commitment to leveraging AI for significant societal impact
