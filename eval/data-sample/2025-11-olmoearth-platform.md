# Introducing OlmoEarth Platform: Powerful open infrastructure for planetary insights

**Date:** November 2025  
**Source:** [Allen Institute for AI](https://allenai.org/blog/olmoearth)

## Summary

OlmoEarth Platform is an open, end-to-end solution designed to transform global satellite and sensor data into real-time Earth insights. It features the OlmoEarth family of state-of-the-art, multimodal foundation models trained on millions of Earth observations totaling approximately 10 terabytes of data.

## Key Features

### End-to-End Solution
Facilitates the entire workflow from raw data acquisition to analysis and deployment, streamlining the process of deriving actionable insights from Earth observation data.

### Frontier Performance
Achieves state-of-the-art results on various benchmarks, ensuring accurate and reliable data to inform decision-making processes.

### Efficiency
Enables continuous monitoring by delivering frequent and reliable results, significantly reducing processing times and costs compared to traditional methods.

## Target Applications

The platform supports organizations and communities addressing critical environmental challenges:
- Food security
- Wildfire resilience
- Conservation efforts

## Early Adopter Results

**Global Mangrove Watch**: Achieved more accurate mapping using just 10% of the data points previously required, completing the process in a matter of days.

## Platform Components

- Continuously updated insights
- Adaptable models
- User-friendly interfaces

The platform empowers users to make informed decisions and respond effectively to global environmental threats without requiring extensive AI or engineering expertise.
