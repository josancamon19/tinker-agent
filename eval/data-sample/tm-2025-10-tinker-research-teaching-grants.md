# Announcing Tinker Research and Teaching Grants

**Date:** October 29, 2025  
**Source:** [Thinking Machines Lab](https://thinkingmachines.ai/blog/tinker-research-and-teaching-grants/)

## Summary

Thinking Machines Lab introduces grants to support research and teaching initiatives using Tinker.

## Grant Program

The program provides resources for:
- Academic researchers conducting AI/ML research
- Educators incorporating Tinker into their curricula
- Students working on thesis projects or research

## Goals

- Lower barriers to advanced model fine-tuning for academia
- Support cutting-edge research in language model training
- Enable hands-on teaching of modern AI techniques
- Foster innovation in the research community

## Eligibility

The grants are designed for researchers and educators who want to leverage Tinker's capabilities for academic purposes.
