# Tinker: Call for Community Projects

**Date:** November 7, 2025  
**Source:** [Thinking Machines Lab](https://thinkingmachines.ai/blog/call-for-community-projects/)

## Summary

Thinking Machines Lab invites the community to submit projects utilizing Tinker for potential feature on the blog.

## Program Details

The lab is seeking interesting and innovative projects that leverage Tinker's fine-tuning capabilities. Selected projects may be:
- Featured on the Thinking Machines blog
- Highlighted in community showcases
- Used as case studies for new users

## How to Participate

Community members working on projects using Tinker are encouraged to submit their work for consideration. This initiative aims to:
- Showcase the diverse applications of Tinker
- Build a community around the platform
- Inspire new users with real-world use cases
